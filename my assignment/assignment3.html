Tesla Model S

The Tesla Model S is an electric luxury sedan produced by Tesla, Inc. The Model S was unveiled in June 2012 and has since become Tesla's flagship model. It is available in several variants including the Standard Range Plus, Long Range, and Performance versions. The Model S features a sleek and modern design, a spacious and comfortable interior, as well as advanced technology such as an infotainment system with a 15-inch touchscreen and full self-driving capability.
